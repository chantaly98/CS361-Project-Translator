Steps for Translation

1. Select the language you want to translate from on the left.

2. Select the language would want to receive the translation in on the right.

3. Then, enter the word, phrase or sentence you would like translated into the left box.

4. Click the "Translate Text" button to get a translated result.


Get Word Count

After translating you can now get a word count of each text. Click the "Get Word Count" button to get a break down of both texts.


Want to learn some random words?

1. Select the language you want on from the right and the left drop downs.

2. Hit the "Random Word" button. A random word should now populate the left text box.

3. Hit the "Translate Text" and receive a random word in your desired language!


Icons

book Save a translation by clicking the bookmark icon.

volume_up To hear the text, click the speaker icon.

swap_horiz Swap the languages by clicking the exchange icon in the middle.
